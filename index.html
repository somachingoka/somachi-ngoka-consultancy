Who I am

I'm a data consultant with years of hands-on experience building data solutions at institutions like Northern Trust and Sigma Computing. I've built and shipped data products used by thousands of people globally.

Alongside my current role, I offer consultancy services to help growing businesses get more from their data, including dashboards, visualisations and full data pipeline architecture, without the overhead of a full-time hire.

I hold a BSc in Information Management for Business from University College London and hold certifications across Microsoft Power BI, Azure, Alteryx, and Databricks.
